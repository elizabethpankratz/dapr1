Department of Psychology, The University of Edinburgh

Academic year 2020-2021

Course overview

Course description

Data analysis for psychology in R 1 or, in short, DAPR1, is your first step on the exciting road of analysing and using data to answer interesting questions in the ways that statisticians, researchers, and data scientists do.

This year-long course is designed to work gently through conceptual content that form the basis of understanding and working with data to perform statistical testing. It will provide you with the foundations in working with data and probability theory, and build to learning about how we make inferences about our hypotheses in psychological research. Concepts are demonstrated in R throughout, taking you from basic calculations to the foundations of data management, plotting and use of simple statistical tests.

The overall aim of the course is to provide you with all the necessary skills to feel confident in the basics of statistics, R and data, before we move on to discuss a broader array of statistical methods in year 2.
